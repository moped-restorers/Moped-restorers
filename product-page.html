Front Bundle

The front bundle of the Tomos A35 old model contains Stainless steel bolts, nuts and washers for the following parts: Handlebar electronics, headlight, indicator lights, front wheel, handlebar.

Each bundle comes with a special crafted bundle manual, so you know exactly where everything goes. This makes it very easy to replace/install.

It is not possible to obtain every bolt, nut and washer in stainless steel. This is because some combinations do not exist. For example, large sizes, fine metric sizes and the correct strength in stainless steel are sometimes difficult to find or do not exist at all.
