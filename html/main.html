About me.

Hello and welcome to MopedRestorers™. My goal is to make it as easy as possible to keep your moped in its original state forever! MopedRestorers™ stems from my passion for mopeds. It all began when I bought my first Tomos A35 and it all got out of hand from that moment. I really liked to work on my Tomos. So after the first one came another Tomos, a Tomos Flexer to be specific. I bought it in a very rough shape and gave it another look, with of course stainless bolts and nuts. It is shown on the picture on the left. I hope I can inspire people and save them a lot of time by offering them my products. And bring the line of doing it on your own, closer. You will learn a lot and have fun at the same time, that is the most beautiful thing. I will stop talking now. I know I´m driving you crazy.

I also filmed a part of my journey and uploaded it on my youtube channel: Martin's Moped. On my channel you can see the entire project of the Tomos Flexer. I really like it, so I'm planning to upload more of my passion on my channel. If you want to take look you can click on the subscribe button on the right.
